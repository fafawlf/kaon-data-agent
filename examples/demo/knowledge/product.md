# Demo SaaS Product

A multi-platform SaaS application with chat-based AI features.

## User Segments
- Free tier: basic features, ad-supported
- Basic tier ($9.99/mo): unlimited chat, no ads
- Premium tier ($29.99/mo): priority model access, advanced features

## Core Metrics
- **DAU**: Count of distinct users with at least one session_start event
- **New Users**: Count of distinct users where signup_date = date
- **Revenue**: Sum of purchase and subscription amounts in USD
- **Chat Sessions**: Count of distinct chat_start events
- **Avg Session Duration**: Average of session_duration_sec for session_start events

## Platforms
- iOS (majority), Android, Web
- Each platform has independent app versions and release cycles

## Key Business Context
- Growth primarily driven by organic and paid acquisition
- Japan and India are emerging markets with different usage patterns
- Android users tend to have lower retention but higher volume
